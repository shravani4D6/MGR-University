About The Tamil Nadu Dr.M.G.R. Medical University

The Tamil Nadu Dr.M.G.R. Medical University is a premier medical institution located in Guindy, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. It is the second-largest health sciences university in India, established to promote excellence in medical education and research.

We oversee a vast network of affiliated medical colleges, dental colleges, and paramedical institutions across the state, ensuring the highest standards of healthcare training.
